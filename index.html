Nurturing the Leaders of tomorrow: Learn About Victor Junior School's Commitment to Uplifting the young ones through, Education.

We have three campuses:

Nabbingo Campus: This is the main Campus with classes form Primary one to Primary seven. It is also home to the Day care section.

Nakasozi Campus: This is called Nakasozi since it is on the road that leads to Nakasozi town but it is in Kiyanja zone. This is home to the Nursery Section, from Baby to Top Class.

Buddo Campus: This is a stand alone branch of with Nursery and early Primary classes. It is located in Buddo Mpangala off Matutu road.

Services Offered.
Education
Curricular & Co-curricular

Educating the pupils being our main goal, we have professional teachers who teach pupils in all sections using the updated school curriculum for Daycare, Nursery and Primary 1 - 7. we also have a UNEB Centre number so we ensure that our P7 pupils sit for their exams at the school premises, a familiar environment.

We take children out for co-curricular activities. We also have leisure (indoor games)

Healthcare Services/

Providing quality healthcare service to improve the overall well-being of our pupils, with a focus on preventive and curative care.

Book Library

Our School Library is well equipped with a variety of Research resources to ensure adequate learning for the pupils

Debates & Quiz

Pupils are encouraged to actively participate in school debates and Quiz. Every year we get pupils who participate in the National Mathematics quiz.

Pupils Residence and School Structures

Our School was built with ample space and rooms to ensure that every class has a number of streams to ensure teacher-pupil interaction at all times. Our Boarders reside at the school Premises designated as Hostels where they are motherly cared for and monitored by their Dorm-Mothers to ensure that a good hygiene is observed.

Computer Literacy & Home Economics

Given the rapid growth towards Technology, we ensure that our pupils are not left behind, We give them a sufficient introduction to Computer and Technology
Equipping young individuals with essential life skills, knowledge, and resources, empowering them to actively participate in community development.
