SCHOOL BACKGROUND

The school was founded in 2011 as a private day primary school with P.1 and P.2 classes only.

In 2012, P.4 was introduced and thereafter promoted to the next class accordingly until 2015 when the pioneers sat for P.L.E.

The founder of the school is Mr. Zziwa Joseph, a seasoned teacher who started as a grade II teacher and upgraded up to a graduate teacher.

The school has a nursery section on a separate piece of land with classes, Nursery and Kindergarten, Pre-primary class is currently housed at the primary campus (main campus).

The school also has a day care facility for children below three years. The mission of the school is “For academic excellence, diligence and development of production skills”.

As a dynamic force for positive change, we strive to create a more resilient and thriving Uganda. Through strategic interventions and collaborative efforts, Victor Junior School Nabbingo remains steadfast in our mission to transform lives, inspire hope, and build a brighter, more equitable future for all.
